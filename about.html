Brand strategy

This site is structured to convert while remaining clinically credible: clear positioning (“advanced & intensive”), a scannable curriculum, and friction reducers (equipment requirements, policies, “what happens next”).

Conversion best practice

  • Above-the-fold CTA + secondary CTA
  • Sticky mobile CTA
  • Fit-check messaging to pre-qualify leads
  • Policy clarity to reduce objections

Business objectives

  • Increase enquiry-to-enrolment rate
  • Reduce admin time with a downloadable course pack
  • Improve lead quality (experience + intent captured)
  • Strengthen trust via clear credential framing

Brand alignment

  • “Counterculture” narrative
  • Natural aesthetics positioning
  • Premium, minimal, glass UI language
  • Clinician-first tone and structure
